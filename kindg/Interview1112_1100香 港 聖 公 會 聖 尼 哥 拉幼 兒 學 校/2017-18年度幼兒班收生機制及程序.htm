2017-18年度幼兒班收生機制及程序

(1) 入學申請資料

派發入學申請表安排

1. 全年派發入學申請表格

2. 不設限額派表

3. 網上下載或到校索取表格

 

收取入學申請表安排

1. 全年收表

2. 收表方法：親臨遞交、郵寄遞交或投入門口收集信箱

3. 遞交申請表時需連同申請者近照兩張相片及出生證明書副本，貼上$1.70郵票之回郵

信封四個並寫上幼兒中文姓名及地址一併交回，不收報名費

 

(2) 校本收生機制

面見安排

1.      於2016年10月20日或之前遞交入學申請表之適齡入學幼兒均安排面見。

2.      面見日期：2016年11月12日(星期六)上午。

 

面見形式

1. 由家長陪同                2. 小組活動及觀察

 

取錄準則

1. 面見表現      2. 社會需要    3. 在校兄姊    4. 原校升讀    5. 教職員子女

 

(3) 註冊安排

通知學位申請結果及安排

1. 申請結果會於11月25日前寄信通知申請人

2. 獲取錄學生：家長須於 2017 年 1 月 13 日至 14 日(「統一註册日期」)內的指定

時間到本幼稚園辦理註册手續，並須提交「2017/18 註冊證」正本及繳交註冊費。

但若家長於註冊後決定為子女轉校，請以書面通知本校。本校會退回相關註冊證，

但註冊費將不獲退還。在取回註冊證後，本校亦不會再為該兒童保留學位。

3.      備取生安排及輪候：「統一註冊日期」後，如有學位，將按備取生表現輪次遞補學位

本校會發出通知，請家長於指定日期到本幼稚園辦理註冊手續，並須提交「2017/18

註冊證」正本及繳交註冊費。

H.K. S.K.H. ST NICHOLAS’ NURSERY SCHOOL

Information on Admission to K1 Classes in the 2017/18 School Year

(1)    Information on Admission Application

Ways to distribute application forms:

1. Application forms are distributed throughout the year.

2. No quota

3. Download from school website or obtain from school in person

 

Return of Application Forms

1. Application forms are received throughout the year

2. Return method: Submit the application form in person / by post / to the drop box at the entrance [together with two recent photographs, a copy of the birth certificate and four envelopes with affixed $1.70 stamp, the Chinese name of the child and the current address]

3. No application fee

 

(2)    School-based Admission Mechanism

Interview Arrangements

1. School-age children who submitted the applications on or before 20 October, 2016 can all be arranged to have interview.

2. Interviews will be conducted in 12 November 2016 (Saturday morning).

 

Format of Interview

1. Parents should accompany their child for the interview.

2. Interview will be conducted in group activities and observation. 

 

Admission Criteria

1. Interview performance

2. Social needs

3. The applicant with sibling(s) currently studying in the kindergarten

4. Current N classes students

5. Our staff’s children

 

(3)    Registration Procedures

Arrangements on announcement of admission results

1. The school will mail notification letters to applicants before 25 November 2016.

2. Successful applicants:

Parents should complete the registration procedures for their child from 13 to 14 January 2017 (“Centralised Registration Dates”) by submitting the original of the “2017/18 RC” to the kindergarten and paying the registration fee HK$1,500. Should parents decide to change school after registration, please notify the kindergarten in writing. The school will return the “2017/18 RC” but the registration fee will not be refunded. Upon obtaining the “2017/18 RC”, the school will no longer keep the school place for the child.

3. Applicants on the waiting list:

If there is any vacancy, the school will offer to applicants according to their performance. 

Parents should complete the registration procedures for their child according to the specified date set by the school. Parents are required to submit the original of the “2017/18 RC” to the kindergarten and pay the registration fee HK$1,500.
